# Demo script

## First ten seconds

1. Show two claim cards with source-verification checks.
2. Say: “Both claims are grounded, but they appear to say the opposite.”
3. Click **Debug disagreement**.
4. Expand only the disagreement-driving rows.
5. Say: “PaperDiff found the methodological difference hidden behind the headlines.”

## Product payoff

Read the verdict, then open one provenance chain from public claim to paper conclusion to exact passage to the method definition responsible for the mismatch.

## Engineering proof

Only after the payoff, open the trace and show parallel extraction, shared schema, deterministic validation, verifier confidence, and honest failure routing.

Before demo day, replace the synthetic fixture with one real open-access pair and prepare two fallbacks. Do not depend on a live external call for the primary demo path.
